What’s changed since last update

Updated to April 16, 2026. Material changes in this refresh: Florida moved to Active after AHCA announced its first round of RHTP procurement opportunities on April 14; Nebraska remains Active and now reflects the Rural Health Workforce Incentive Program opening on April 15; New Jersey remains Active, but the Community Health Workers deadline has passed and the live doula integration opportunity continues through April 27; Oregon remains Active and now lists the official Catalyst Awards solicitation deadline of May 26; and the events section was refreshed against April 16 so past events and live Oklahoma engagement dates display correctly. Most other state pages showed no material public change from the prior version.

How to use this tracker

Click the State, Region, or Status column header to sort. Active means a live application or package-level RFA or RFP is open now. Recently closed means a recent cycle just ended but is still useful for partner mapping. Forecasted means an official state source has announced an upcoming window or release period. Monitor means the state is strategically relevant, but no public competitive dates were found on official RHTP pages as of the verification date.
